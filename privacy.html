Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information from our users. The images and prompts you provide are sent to the Google Gemini API for processing and are not stored on our servers. Your data is handled in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy, which governs the use of the Gemini API.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your uploaded images and text prompts are used solely for the purpose of generating an edited image via the Gemini API. We do not use this data for any other purpose, such as training models or advertising. All processing is transient and occurs in real-time.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data during transmission to the Gemini API using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS). However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Our service relies on the Google Gemini API. Your use of our service is also subject to Google's terms and privacy policies. We encourage you to review them to understand how your data is handled by Google.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
